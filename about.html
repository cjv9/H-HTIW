How the Internet Works, and its History

This site aims to tell the story of the Internet, from its conception in the late 1900's, to the revolutionary implementation we have today, of the Cloud, Kubernetes, and Docker

Why it Matters

Technology is all around us, and it's easy to forget that not that long ago, tools like Google, YouTube, Instagram and Netflix did not exsist, or work the way they do today. To be able to fully appreciate the commodities we have today, we must learn of how they came to be.
